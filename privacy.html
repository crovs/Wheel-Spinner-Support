Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

Spinny - Spin the Wheel ("we", "us", or "our") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information when you use our mobile application, including through Google AdMob and RevenueCat integration.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information Collected via AdMob

  • Device Identifiers: We use Google AdMob to serve ads. AdMob may collect device-specific information such as advertising identifiers, IP address, device model, operating system version, and app usage data. Google Privacy Policy.
  • Advertising Interaction: Ad impressions and interactions (clicks events) are collected to personalize ads and measure performance.

1.2 Information Collected via RevenueCat

  • Purchase Information: If you make in-app purchases, RevenueCat collects purchase events, subscription status, and transaction identifiers to provide entitlement management and subscription services. RevenueCat Privacy Policy.
  • User Identifiers: A unique identifier (e.g., RevenueCat user ID) is used to track subscriptions and entitlements.

1.3 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: If you choose to provide your email for support or newsletters.
  • Support Requests: Content of your messages when you contact us for support.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate and improve the app’s features and performance.
  • To serve personalized advertisements via AdMob.
  • To manage and verify in-app purchases and subscriptions via RevenueCat.
  • To respond to your support requests and communicate updates.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We may share your information with the following third parties:

  • Google AdMob: For ad serving, personalization, and analytics.
  • RevenueCat: For subscription and purchase management.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law or to protect our rights.

4. Your Choices and Rights

  • Advertising Opt-out: You can opt out of personalized ads by disabling Advertising ID for your device or by visiting About Ads Preferences.
  • Subscription Management: You can manage or cancel subscriptions through your device settings or through RevenueCat’s customer portal.
  • Data Access and Deletion: You may request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us at the email below.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information as long as necessary to provide the app’s services, comply with legal obligations, or resolve disputes.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice in the app or via email.
