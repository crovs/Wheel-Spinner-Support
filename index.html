Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add or remove segments on the wheel?

Tap the “Edit Segments” button on the main screen. You can add, delete, or rename segments. Don’t forget to save your changes!

Can I customize the wheel’s colors?

Yes! Go to Settings → Appearance. Choose from preset themes or pick your own colors using the color picker.

How do I share the result?

After spinning the wheel, tap the Share icon. You can share via social media, email, or copy the link to your clipboard.

Is there a way to save my favorite wheels?

Absolutely. While viewing a wheel, tap the “Save” (star) icon. Access all saved wheels from the My Wheels tab.

Troubleshooting

  • App won’t launch: Restart your device, ensure you’re on the latest version, or reinstall the app.
  • Spin button unresponsive: Check that you have at least 2 segments added. Try toggling “Enable Animations” in Settings.
  • Crashes during spin: Clear app cache (Settings → Storage → Clear Cache) or reinstall.
  • Notifications not working: Make sure notifications are enabled for Wheel Spinner in your device’s settings.
